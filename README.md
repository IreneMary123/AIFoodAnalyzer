Project Description
NutriVision AI is an AI-powered web application that analyzes uploaded food images to estimate their nutritional content and provides personalized health suggestions based on the user‚Äôs fitness goal.

The Problem statement
People often struggle to track what they eat and estimate nutritional content. Manual tracking is tedious, and existing apps are not always accurate or convenient.

The Solution
NutriVision AI allows users to upload a photo of their meal and instantly receive calorie estimates, macronutrient breakdowns, and health suggestions tailored to their fitness goal.

Features
List the key features of your project:

- AI-powered Food Recognition: Upload a photo and identify the food automatically.  
- Nutritional Estimation: Get calories, protein, carbs, and fat for the recognized food.  
- Health Suggestions: Tailored suggestions based on goals like weight loss, muscle gain, diabetic-friendly, or balanced diet.  
- Clean UI:** Modern, aesthetic interface with smooth gradients and card design.  
- Quick Analysis: Fast feedback for easy daily tracking.
